Interactive Economic Labs

A suite of tools for learning Mathematical Economics.

1. Economic Model Builder

Explore the building blocks of economic models by visualizing and manipulating various mathematical functions.

2. Equilibrium Analyzer

Analyze market equilibrium by adjusting supply and demand, and see the impact of taxes and subsidies.

3. The Complete Elasticity Lab

Master Price, Income, and Cross-Price elasticity. Understand the crucial difference between slope and elasticity.

4. Firm's Optimization Dashboard

Visualize a firm's profit maximization strategy by analyzing total and marginal costs and revenues.

5. Constrained Optimization

Solve utility maximization and output maximization problems using indifference curves and budget constraints.

6. Production & Input-Output

Explore production functions and analyze inter-industry dependencies with the Leontief Input-Output model.

7. Surplus & Linear Programming

Analyze welfare with consumer/producer surplus and solve optimization problems with the graphical LP method.
